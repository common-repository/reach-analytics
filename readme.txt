== Description ==

Reach Analytics provides:

= Traffic Insights =
See your audience segmented based on interests, location, gender, age etc like never before with our cookie sync technology.
Create audience segment like food, travel and promote your related content directly to these audiences.

= Extend Your Blog Reach =
Built in capability to target your customer and bring them back for the new content. You can also target new users on Facebook to increase your website traffic.

= Audience Extension =
Target your audience even after you have sold out of inventory and add a new revenue channel over and above the native ads.
Audience extension helps leverage your users to generate incremental revenue. This highly innovative solution enables publishers to further monetise their audience segments.

= Dedicated Support =
We have an entire team of happiness engineers ready to help you. Ask your questions on our contact form http://adreach.io/#contact or email at care@adreach.io.

== Installation ==
Install Reach Analytics via the plugin directory, or by uploading the files manually to your server. Activating Reach Analytics plugin will automatically prepare a dashboard on www.adreach.io. Please check your wordpress admin account email for how to access the dashboard.
